About Cards for Normals

We're tired of credit card advice that assumes you want to become a points expert

Why We Started This

The credit card world is full of conflicting advice, endless Reddit threads, and "experts" trying to convince you that you need 17 different cards to optimize every purchase category.

But here's the thing: most people just want a straightforward answer. They want to know which card makes sense for their actual life, not for some theoretical optimal-spending scenario that requires a spreadsheet to manage.

We built Cards for Normals because we believe good financial advice should be simple, honest, and focused on what actually matters in real life.

Our Philosophy

Credit cards are tools, not hobbies. A good card should make your life easier, not more complicated. Sometimes the "optimal" choice on paper is terrible in practice if it requires constant mental overhead.

We're not here to turn you into a points maximizer or convince you to apply for cards with $500 annual fees. We're here to help you find the card that fits your spending, your goals, and your tolerance for complexity.
