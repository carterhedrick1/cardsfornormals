Practical Recommendation

The American Express Platinum is best suited for frequent travelers who can maximize its premium perks. With a $695 annual fee, this card makes financial sense if you:

  • Travel 4+ times per year and can use multiple credits effectively
  • Have airport lounge access needs worth the Centurion and Priority Pass benefits
  • Book flights and hotels directly to earn meaningful 5X points
  • Value transfer partners for maximizing point redemptions

Perks

Luxury benefits and exclusive access with the American Express Platinum:

  • Fine Hotels & Resorts program
  • Car rental elite status
  • Concierge service
  • Event ticket presales

Protections
