Practical Recommendation

The Citi Double Cash® Card is best suited for people who want simple, flat-rate cash back. With a $0 annual fee, this card makes financial sense if you:

  • Want flat, effortless cash back – prefer 2% everywhere with no categories to track
  • Avoid annual fees – seek a $0-AF daily driver
  • Primarily spend in the U.S. – won't be hit by the 3% foreign transaction fee

Unique advantage over other 2% cards: Double Cash gives 5% total on hotels, car rentals, and attractions booked via Citi Travel (it's the usual 1% + 1% plus an extra 3%). Cards like Wells Fargo Active Cash have no similar portal booster.

Perks

Key benefits and access with the Citi Double Cash® Card:

  • 5% total on hotels, car rentals & attractions when booked through Citi Travel
  • ThankYou® Points earning (redeemable for cash back)
  • 0% intro APR for 18 months on balance transfers (then variable APR)
  • If you also hold an eligible Citi Strata Premier/Elite, you can transfer points to airline/hotel partners

Protections

Insurance and security features included:

  • Purchase Protection — covers new purchases for 120 days against damage or theft
  • Extended Warranty — extends manufacturer's warranty by up to 2 years
  • Zero Liability Protection — no liability for unauthorized charges
