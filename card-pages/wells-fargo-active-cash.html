Practical Recommendation

The Wells Fargo Active Cash® is best suited for people who want simple, flat-rate cash back. With a $0 annual fee, this card makes financial sense if you:

  • Want flat, effortless cash back – prefer 2% everywhere with no categories to track
  • Pay cell phone bill on a card – can leverage the built-in phone protection
  • Avoid annual fees – seek a $0-AF daily driver
  • Primarily spend in the U.S. – won't be hit by the 3% foreign transaction fee

The protections on this card are pretty good and can save real money, but most of the perks are not that great. We wouldn't recommend the card for any of the perks below. It is a good card for people who want a simple, flat-rate cash back card with no annual fee.

Perks

Key benefits and access with the Wells Fargo Active Cash®:

  • Visa Signature® Concierge access
  • Visa Signature® Luxury Hotel Collection benefits
  • Travel & Emergency Assistance services
  • Roadside Dispatch®
  • 0% intro APR for 12 months on purchases & qualifying balance transfers (then variable APR)

Protections

Insurance and security features included:

  • Cell Phone Protection — up to $600 per claim, $1,200 per 12 months; $25 deductible; pay your monthly wireless bill with the card
  • Auto Rental Collision Damage Waiver — secondary in the U.S. (primary abroad), up to $50,000; max 15 days in U.S./31 days abroad; certain countries excluded

Hybrid (Optional): Convert Cash to Miles

Active Cash is a simple 2% cash-back card first. If you're more advanced with travel rewards, you can pair it with Wells Fargo Autograph® or Autograph Journey℠, move your rewards into that account, and then transfer to airline/hotel partners for potential outsized value.

This is an expert strategy—best if you're planning award trips and willing to learn the ropes. If you want to pursue this approach, check out our Level 2 Travel Learning section.
