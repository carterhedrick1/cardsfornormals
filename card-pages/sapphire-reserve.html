Practical Recommendation

The Chase Sapphire Reserve is best suited for frequent travelers who can maximize its premium perks. With a $795 annual fee, this card makes financial sense if you:

  • Travel 3+ times per year and can use the $300 travel credit
  • Have airport lounge access needs worth the Priority Pass benefit
  • Spend heavily on travel & dining to earn meaningful 3X points
  • Value transfer partners for maximizing point redemptions

Perks

Premium travel benefits and access with the Chase Sapphire Reserve:

  • Priority Pass lounges
  • The Edit hotel collection
  • Exclusive Tables dining
  • StubHub/Viagogo credits
  • Primary car rental insurance

Protections

Insurance and security features included:

  • Extended warranty protection
  • Fraud protection
  • Trip delay insurance
  • Primary car rental coverage
  • Zero liability protection
