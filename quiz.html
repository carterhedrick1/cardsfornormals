Smart choice! Now let me teach you something most people don't know: there are actually three different types of cash back cards, and picking the wrong one can cost you hundreds of dollars a year.

What type of cash back card fits your life?

Flat Rate (1.5-2% on everything)
Simple and consistent. Great if you don't want to think about categories.
Bonus Categories (3-6% on specific things)
Higher rewards on groceries, gas, dining, etc. More money if you shop the categories.
Rotating Categories (5% that changes quarterly)
Highest potential rewards, but you have to remember to activate and track categories.

üí° The math that matters:

A 2% flat rate card beats a 5% category card if you only spend $2,000/year in those bonus categories. Most people overestimate how much they spend in specific categories!
