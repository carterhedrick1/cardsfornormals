How we handle your information (spoiler: we collect very little)

We don't collect much information about you. We don't sell your data. We don't track you across the internet. We just want to help you understand credit cards without being creepy about it.

1. What Information We Collect

Information You Give Us Directly

  • Email: If you contact us, we'll have your email address and whatever you tell us in your message
  • Feedback: If you share experiences with cards or suggestions for the site
  • Calculator Data: When you use our tools, the information stays in your browser - we don't store it

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Basic Analytics: We use simple analytics to understand which pages people visit and how they use the site
  • Technical Info: Your browser type, device type, and general location (like "United States" not your home address)
  • No Personal Tracking: We don't create profiles of you or track your behavior across other websites

2. What We Don't Collect

We specifically avoid collecting:

  • Your real name (unless you tell us in an email)
  • Your address, phone number, or other contact details
  • Your credit score, income, or financial information
  • Your social security number or other sensitive identifiers
  • Your browsing history on other websites
  • Detailed location data (we don't need to know your exact whereabouts)

3. How We Use Your Information

If You Email Us

We use your email and message to:

  • Respond to your questions
  • Improve our content based on your feedback
  • Understand what topics people are confused about

Analytics Data

We use website analytics to:

  • Understand which content is most helpful
  • Fix pages that aren't working well
  • Decide what new content to create
  • Make the site faster and easier to use

4. Who We Share Information With

The Short Answer: Almost Nobody

  • Analytics Provider: We use a privacy-focused analytics service that doesn't track individuals
  • Email Service: If you email us, our email provider (like Gmail) will have that data
  • Web Hosting: Our hosting provider stores the website but doesn't have access to personal information
  • Legal Requirements: We'd comply with valid legal requests, but we don't have much data to share anyway

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your information to anyone
  • We don't share data with credit card companies
  • We don't participate in advertising networks that track you
  • We don't give your information to data brokers

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Basic cookies that make the website work properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Simple analytics to understand site usage (no personal tracking)
  • No Advertising Cookies: We don't use cookies for ads or cross-site tracking
  • Your Choice: You can disable cookies in your browser if you prefer

6. Data Security

We protect information by:

  • Using secure hosting with encryption
  • Not storing sensitive information we don't need
  • Keeping email correspondence secure
  • Following basic security best practices

However, no website is 100% secure. Don't email us sensitive financial information like account numbers or social security numbers.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Access: Ask what information we have about you (probably just your email if you contacted us)
  • Delete: Ask us to delete your information
  • Correct: Tell us if we have incorrect information
  • Opt Out: Stop using the site anytime you want
  • Browser Settings: Control cookies and tracking through your browser

8. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for adults. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you're under 13, please don't use the site or contact us. If we learn we've collected information from a child, we'll delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We might update this policy occasionally. If we make significant changes, we'll update the date at the top and might mention it on the site. We won't make the policy worse for your privacy without notice.

10. International Users

This site is based in the United States and primarily intended for US users. If you're outside the US and use the site, your information might be transferred to and processed in the US.

11. Contact Us About Privacy

Questions about this privacy policy? Want to know what information we have about you? Want us to delete your information? Email us at hello@cardsfornormals.com.
