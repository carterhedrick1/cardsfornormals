Quick Answers to Common Questions

Do you make money from card recommendations?
No. We don't have affiliate relationships with credit card companies. Our recommendations are based purely on what we think makes sense for different situations.
Can you help me with my specific credit situation?
We can point you toward the right type of card and explain the trade-offs, but we can't provide personalized financial advice. For specific credit issues, consider talking to a nonprofit credit counselor.
Why don't you cover [specific card]?
We focus on cards that make sense for most people in common situations. If there's a card you think we should cover, let us know! We're always looking to expand our coverage of genuinely useful options.
How often do you update your recommendations?
We review our recommendations regularly as card terms change. If you notice outdated information, please let us know so we can fix it quickly.
